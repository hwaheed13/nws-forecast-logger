✓ FORMULA 1: entrainment_temp_diff = atm_925mb_temp_mean - obs_latest_temp
  Source columns:
  - atm_925mb_temp_mean (atmospheric data, populated by open_meteo_client.py)
  - obs_latest_temp (live KNYC observation, updated every hour)
  Computation:
  - Check both exist and are not NaN
  - Subtract obs from 925mb
  - Round to 1 decimal
  Result precision: 0.1°F
  Safety: Handles missing source data gracefully → NaN

✓ FORMULA 2: marine_containment = obs_kjfk_vs_knyc / atm_bl_height_max
  Source columns:
  - obs_kjfk_vs_knyc (KJFK temp - KNYC temp, from synoptic.py)
  - atm_bl_height_max (boundary layer height max, from open_meteo.py)
  Computation:
  - Check both exist and are not NaN
  - Check BL height > 0 (critical safety check)
  - Divide gradient by depth
  - Round to 6 decimals (handles ratio precision)
  Result range: typically -0.01 to +0.01 (ratio of degrees to meters)
  Safety: Explicit division by zero check

✓ FORMULA 3: inland_strength = mean(obs_kteb, obs_kcdw, obs_ksmq) - mm_mean
  Source columns:
  - obs_kteb_temp, obs_kcdw_temp, obs_ksmq_temp (Synoptic ASOS stations)
  - mm_mean (multimodel consensus, from open_meteo.py)
  Computation:
  - Collect all available inland temps (handles 1, 2, or 3 available)
  - Check mm_mean exists and is not NaN
  - Calculate mean of inland temps (skipna in DataFrame)
  - Subtract mm_mean
  - Round to 1 decimal
  Result precision: 0.1°F
  Safety: Handles sparse inland data (not all 3 stations always available)
